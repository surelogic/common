SureLogic tool licensing

You need a license to use the SureLogic tools.

We have three ways to license our tools: 
• Community
• Trial
• Enterprise

Enterprise licenses are purchased at our on-line store or you can ask for a quote at info@surelogic.com. Ask for a quote if you have a special situation, e.g., you don't have Internet access.

Once you have a license, please read the download and installation instructions for how to install and activate your license in Eclipse.

Community (Free)

The SureLogic community license is free for individual developers, open source projects, academic research, education, and small professional teams. You qualify unless you require an enterprise license (see below) due to whom you work for.

This license provides free, full-featured JSure, Flashlight, and Sierra tools for Java and Android development in the Eclipse IDE and at the command-line with limited support (on-line). An internet connection is required for license activation and renewal.

Enterprise

An enterprise license is required for most commercial and government use of the SureLogic tools.

When do you need an enterprise license?

Commercial use: If you use the tools on commercial code and your company has an annual revenue of greater than $1M (or equivalent) and/or your company has more than 5 people using the SureLogic tools.

Government use: If you use the tools on any code within a government organization.

You can purchase a license at our on-line store or ask for a quote at info@surelogic.com. If you are purchasing licenses for more than 25 developers (50 for Sierra-only use), a quote is recommended.

We suggest you get a 45-day trial license (see above) to "try before you buy".

Purchase Enterprise Licenses

You can purchase a license for all the tools or just the ones you need. If you have any special needs, for example you do not have an Internet connection to activate your trial license or you require phone support — please contact us at info@surelogic.com.

If you need to use a PO and have us invoice you — please contact us at info@surelogic.com.
